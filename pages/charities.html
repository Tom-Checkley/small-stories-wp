Our first event on Sunday September 11th at The Lantern, Colston Hall will raise funds for free counselling service, The Harbour.

About The Harbour

The Harbour provides professional counselling, free of charge, to people affected by life-threatening illness. This includes people with an illness, their carers and loved ones, and people who have been bereaved.

Some of the people they have supported will tell you their story at the event.

There are no fees for our services; we cover the costs of the sessions through fundraising activities and grants from our partners such as Bristol CCG, Macmillan, local grant-makers, and individual donors.

Our vision is that everyone in Bristol has access to high quality counselling to support them with the emotional pain that comes with life-threatening illness.

We support around 300 people each year through 1:1 counselling, couples counselling, and group therapy.
